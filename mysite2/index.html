ABOUT THE JANN

Jann Mardenborough is a famous British racing driver who made his way into the world of professional motorsport through an incredible adventure. Starting with karting at a karting track in Cardiff as a child, he quickly discovered his talent and passion for racing. However, not having the financial support to further develop in this field forced him to change direction.

In 2011, Mardenborough took part in the GT Academy, a competition organized by Nissan and Sony. He won out of 90,000 entrants and got the chance to move from virtual racing on PlayStation to the real world of motorsports. This win opened the door to professional racing, where he impressed with his abilities and proved to be competitive.

Mardenborough quickly advanced in various series, including participation in GT, Formula 3, GP3 and other championships. He impressed spectators and experts alike with his driving skills, making his debut in various racing classes and earning a reputation as one of the most talented drivers of his generation.
